Sending a message:
To send someone a message, follow these simple steps:
1. Navigate to the person's profile page.
2. On their profile, look for the 'Message' button.
3. Click on the 'Message' button to open the chat window.
4. Type your message in the chat input field.
5. Press 'Send' or hit Enter to send your message.
You're all set! The person will receive your message, and you can start chatting with them.

Blocking a user:
If you encounter someone you want to block, you can easily do so from their profile page. Here's how:
1. Go to the profile page of the person you wish to block.
2. On their profile, you should see a 'Block' option or button.
3. Click on the 'Block' option to initiate the blocking process.
4. Confirm your action when prompted to block the user.
Once you've completed these steps, the user will be blocked, and they will no longer be able to interact with you or see your profile.
